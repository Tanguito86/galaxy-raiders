## 7. Success Criteria for External Validation

### Validation SUCCESS

| Criterion | Evidence |
|-----------|----------|
| **Replay retention** | > 60% of testers play 3+ sessions voluntarily |
| **Score engagement** | > 50% of score-play testers achieve multiplier > ×1.3 |
| **Voluntary replay** | > 70% of testers answer "yes" to "want to play again?" |
| **Memorable encounters** | > 4/5 bosses described by mechanic, not color |
| **Healthy frustration** | > 70% of deaths classified as FAIR (player's fault) |
| **Adaptation curves** | Death count decreases across runs for > 60% of testers |

### Validation FAILURE (Would Require HC-CAL Reopen)

| Criterion | Evidence |
|-----------|----------|
| **Replay collapse** | > 40% of testers play only 1 session |
| **Systemic unfairness** | > 30% of deaths classified as CHEAP |
| **Score irrelevance** | 0/5 score-play testers engage with any scoring system |
| **Fatigue toxicity** | > 50% of sessions end early with exhaustion complaints |
| **Readability breakdown** | > 30% of deaths attributed to visibility issues |
| **Recovery collapse** | > 50% of first deaths lead to run abandonment |
